# Connect your Social Apps to Livefyre Identity


>1. Create an App.
>1. Navigate to ** `Studio` ** > ** `Settings` ** > ** `Integration Settings` **> ** `Livefyre Identity` **.
>1. Enter the required App credentials for the App you created.
